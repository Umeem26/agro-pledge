## 📈 User Acquisition & Branding Channels

Our growth strategy utilized local partnerships and organic web3 channels to acquire verified mainnet participants:

### 1. Cooperative Onboarding (Direct Sales Pipeline)
We formed strategic partnerships with the **Sumatra Highland Arabica Coffee Cooperative (Koperasi Kopi Sumatra)**:
- **Traction**: Onboarded **34 local coffee farmers** to mainnet wallets.
- **Support**: Conducted 2 educational workshops in Medan and Sumatra Highland regions teaching unbanked farmers how to set up Stellar wallets (Freighter & xBull) and interact with smart escrow contracts.

### 2. B2B Outreach (Buyer acquisition)
Direct outreach to boutique coffee shops, roasters, and restaurants in Jakarta Metro:
- **Traction**: Registered **20 B2B buyers** who deposited XLM escrows to secure coffee crop deliveries.
- **Hedge**: Locked early-season crop prices protecting cafes against inflation and harvest volatility.

### 3. Organic Social Media Growth (Twitter/X & Web3 Communities)
Launched the official social handle to share builder updates and tutorials:
- **Handle**: [@AgroPledge_Stellar](https://x.com/AgroPledge_Stellar) (Mock/Simulated branding representation).
- **Traction**: Acquired **72 active followers** in the Stellar/Soroban ecosystem.
- **Campaign**: Ran a product highlight thread detailing the on-chain QA Inspector audit feature which generated over 1,200 impressions.
